6. Visual Styling Recommendations
	•	Font weights: Use medium or bold for titles, light for descriptions
	•	Icons: Avoid emojis – use line icons like checkmarks, stars or tags (Lucide or Heroicons)
	•	Colors: Beige, warm gray, soft white. Use your primary color only for highlight buttons.
	•	Animations: Subtle entrance fade-ins or button ripple effect on hover
	•	Responsive behavior: On mobile, show one card per row with auto stacking
